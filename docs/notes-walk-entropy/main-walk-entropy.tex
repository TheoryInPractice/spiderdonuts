\abstract{
Research journal on the walk-entropy problem, which started from Blair Sullivan and I considering a conjecture from Michele Benzi regarding the relationship of the walk-regularity of a graph to the diagonal of a function of the graph's adjacency matrix.

Since NCSU undergraduate Eric Whorton joined the project, we've identified a handful more specific deceptive graphs, and eventually proved the existence of infinite families of deceptive graphs.
